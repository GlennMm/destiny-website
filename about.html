Destiny Achievers Group of schools is a privately-owned group of schools that constitute of day and boarding schools. The group of schools was founded in 2018 as a private educational institution. Located on the urban fringes of Harare metropolitan city, Destiny Achievers Group of Schools is administratively located in Goromonzi District under Mashonaland East Province.

A rapidly growing school since its inception in 2018 , Destiny Achievers now has two campuses; one at Glenforest (Domboshawa) and another at Cernery Park (Ushewokunze) in Harare. The Glenforest campus is a private boarding institution that is made up of both a junior and a senior school. The senior school offers a dynamic Cambridge international curriculum from Form 1 to Form 6 while the junior school caters for the Zimsec curriculum from ECD A to Grade 7.

Vision

To be the leading Education Center in Zimbabwe and contribute towards the performance of learners in Maths, Science, Technology and Arts.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an engaging educational environment which surrounds the child in a community of learners who acknowledge and believe in the unique yet dynamic nature of the human spirit and strive to foster and nurture the individual through meaningful and challenging opportunities in Maths, Science, Business, and Communication while collectively experiencing a renaissance of the mind.
